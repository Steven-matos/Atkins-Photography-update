About

Hi, I'm Cedric. A Florida native photographer based in Orlando. I'm self-taught with a sincere passion for all things photographic,genuine people, and beautiful spaces. I love keeping things simple in life as I find it always produces the best results with the least stress. No matter the size of the project I always bring the same passion and dedication to each individual job and it's my priority to make sure I have captured your memories. Let's connect and make awesome work together.
